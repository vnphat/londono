About

Tatiana Londono is an ambitious, hard-working real estate broker who struggles to balance family life with work, and do it all with style. Driven by her formidable work ethic and self-discipline, she was determined to succeed on her own and launched the Londono Realty Group in June 2007. She had a vision of a real estate agency bringing something fresh and energized to an old and tired business. In a few short years, Londono Realty Group has grown from its original handful of agents to its current roster of over 100 and has become one of the most successful independent offices in the country. Specializing in residential properties, Tatiana and her dynamic team offer a professional buying and selling experience in the Greater Montreal area.

Her bright smile and blond curls caught the eyes of a television production company, who offered her a position as the star of her own HGTV hit show The Property Shop. The series mirrors Tatiana’s experiences starting out on her own and provides viewers with an inside look at what it takes to build a successful real estate agency.

With her drive, down to earth business sense, combined with impressive sales skills, Tatiana dreams big! She hopes to turn Londono Realty Group into a multi-million dollar empire with agencies all over the country, if not the world!
